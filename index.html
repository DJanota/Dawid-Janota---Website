About Me

Who I am?

Dawid Janota

Web Developer

Hello! My name is Dawid. I am Junior Web Developer and an experienced Registered General Nurse. For several years I have been fascinated with surgery and new technologies that are used, inter alia, in medicine. I am particularly interested in software created for devices aimed at improving quality or saving lives. Therefore, I decided to explore the secrets of programming and that's how my new remarkable passion was born. I decided that I would like to develop and work as a Web Developer. To this end, I completed Bootcamp Web Developer, which is the first step to make my own plans come true. I'm currently developing my skills related to JavaScript programming language. I plan to study engineering in the field of Computer Science. In the future, I would like to gain competences that allow me to work as a Full Stack Developer. In my free time I like hiking in the mountains, playing football, breeding exotic birds and traveling on a motorcycle.
